ABOUT US

A world-class salon and spa experience in your neighbourhood.

Jintin Luxurious Unisex Salon and Spa was established in 2020 with three guiding ideas in mind: passion, creativity, and undying love for beauty. At Jintin, we take pleasure in working with our customers one-on-one during their entire visit. This way, you'll receive a highly tailored approach to your hair journey in a relaxing salon setting.

Our Spas are the ideal places to come if you're looking for some natural relaxation and pampering. Our highly educated stylists, beauty therapists, and aesthetic technicians have years of expertise in their respective professions and can be considered industry experts at any of our locations. At our unisex Salon Madurai, the staff are trained enough to offer suggestions for hairstyles, treatments, and therapies that suit you.

We constantly endeavour to stay updated on new treatments and advancements to provide you with the best service possible. We blend elegance with simplicity to immerse our clients in a beautiful and intimate environment.

We provide some of India's most incredible bridal makeup, from modern mehndi looks to romantic wedding makeup. On your big day, we have a team of experienced specialists to provide you with the most fabulous wedding makeover. With industry experts, we provide one of the best keratin smoothening treatments in India. Our services are currently available in Chennai, Madurai and Ahmedabad. Expect our services in other cities shortly.
